Medications fill
Lack in the body.
And the lack of what?
Not so good,
Not those goals...
 
Chemical cause
They will be found in the laboratory.
Pills will give
Or they will rise
Outpatient.
 
Choose: 
Meaningful habits
Wisdom to acquire,
Whether to lay out the shelves
Pharmaceutical poison.
