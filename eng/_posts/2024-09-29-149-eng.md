The Wolf and the Goat — 
Aggressor and victim
Sufferers of tears,
Satisfied and dead.
 
Goat and cabbage —
Vegetarianism. 
Humanity of humanity,
Gluttony and drunkenness.
 
Cabbage and wolf —
Mutual indifference.
The rapist was silent.
Does not bite or choke.
