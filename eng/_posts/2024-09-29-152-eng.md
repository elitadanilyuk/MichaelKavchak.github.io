No need to answer immediately 
Let it chew.
In order not to cause disgust,
Better to wait.
 
In haste — 
Mistake. 
Excessive tension. 
Broken arrow.
You lose a friend.
 
Don't sleep alone 
And don't drive him — 
Allow the words 
Achieve your own.
