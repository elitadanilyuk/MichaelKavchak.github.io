Oh, how many geniuses
In cemeteries. 
Waiting for prizes 
In the heavens. 

Drowned by contemporaries,
Smiled and muzzled,
Hanged and drowned
They are honored by the Creator.

“Stripped” and forgotten,
There were no names,
Perfect in trouble...
Behind them is the idea.
