What did you, Dmitry, do?
Changed the person. 
You changed her mind, 
Calm threw me away. 

He harnessed me to the phone, 
Press buttons only. 
To which such extracts 
You call unintentionally. 

I know, I know, it's not you.
You just prayed.
Let's go to one goal,
Let God be proud.
