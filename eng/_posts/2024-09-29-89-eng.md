Loving the egoist —
Requires its own. 
Substitutes the tail — 
Hold on to him.

You love yourself —
Get ready to serve.
Till then you know
Pleasure to live.

Enslaving others —
Slavery itself.
There is more responsibility.
Selfishness is sacrilege.
