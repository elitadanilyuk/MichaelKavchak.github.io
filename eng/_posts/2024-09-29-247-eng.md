At three thirty three
Awakened,
Until the fourth, it is confirmed.
By half past five I am touched —
Consciousness is fixed.

Conscience beckoned
In a dream —
Word sifted
TO-BI.

Edited to the fifth,
Keep sleeping.
From fate 
Taken for carts
Wait for the results.
