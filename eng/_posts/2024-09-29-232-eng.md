We are just emigrants,
Peaceful settlers.
They talk about us “invaders”
Local foreigners. 

There are not so many of them,
Empty territories.
We go to live 
The ruins are unplowed.

We are being killed here 
Opponents of civilization. 
Therefore, we drive
Savages on the reservation.
