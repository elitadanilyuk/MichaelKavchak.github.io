To love Russian —
To live in Russia. 
To consider yourself a Ukrainian — 
Speak Ukrainian. 

Go to someone else's —
Stranger and take away.
As if you were —
But by whom — I forgot. 

There was no language — 
Ukraine has fallen. 
What they fought for,
Blood spilled?
