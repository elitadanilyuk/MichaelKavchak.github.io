Flags are getting smaller —
They became tired. 
Indifference first 
Runs away from moans.

Tired of depression
We want victories.
Forgotten aggression —
Enough anxiety. 

Neighbor's hut 
It's already burning.
And I need to plow —
The field is waiting.
