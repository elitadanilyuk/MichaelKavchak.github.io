God is jealous of traitors,
Cruel to evil. 
Stops attackers 
Strengthens the weak.

He took sacrifices, 
Until he sacrificed himself,
Whole burn canceled,
He doesn't want dead. 

No dead love,
No dead good, 
No cunning in the word,
When the soul is dead.
