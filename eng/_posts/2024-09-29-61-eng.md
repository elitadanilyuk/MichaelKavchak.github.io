Energy pours out 
In the bowl of eternity, 
Everyone is expected
Get rid of misery.

Even in evil
Looking for the best.
If you do not eat, then to yourself
Greater value.

Eternity takes
From the living and the dead
From whole and ground.
From whom he receives,
He draws from her.
