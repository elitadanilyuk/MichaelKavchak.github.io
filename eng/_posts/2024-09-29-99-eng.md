Can be envied
The old emigration,
Advise others 
Dissident shares.

And the tragedy of resettlement
Not just in nostalgia.
He is in translation 
In the conditions of a stranger.

Do not envy the persecuted,
What life has saved.
He is uncontrollable from the pressure
A close race.
