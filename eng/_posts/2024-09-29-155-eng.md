Easily recognize the enemy
Among the enemies. 
But it is expensive 
How I met
 
On the road is flat
Among the countrymen.
Bigger than relatives
There are no enemies.
 
Than close family 
Than close neighbors 
Knife in Tandem*...
 
Who goes next door.
____________
*Tandem is an e-app for communication by correspondence and in group conversations.
