Do not believe in Ascension?
And in reincarnation? 
And in a life without aging
Or in levitation?

Do not believe? I'm up.
And returned to earth.
Nalevitiated
And reincarnated
Got younger for sure. 

You think I'm mad 
Would you say a sect like this? 
I just landed
Just got off the plane.
