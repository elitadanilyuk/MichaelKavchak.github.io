Born to suffer
To learn to thank.
So I will love 
Instead of mourning. 

Thank you for the benefits, 
Thank you for the misery. 
For someone's respect, 
Echid for grimaces.

For beatings and medicines
With love as a sign
I have to rejoice. 
Thank you, God, thank you.
