Saint Nicholas is back.
Santa Claus is enough.
Narugu scoop remember, —
Parental suffering asks.

Communal faith 
Thought would disturb.
From Christmas only the shadow — 
And will not suffocate

To some Santa Claus,
Per Noel to someone.
Why did it seem to us
Godless “Methel”?
