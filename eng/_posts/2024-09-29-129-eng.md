Afraid of criticism 
Because you are looking for glory.
To avoid shame,
Are you listening, isn't it “Bravo!”
 
To chase glory — 
She runs away,
Hiding from her —
Catching up. 
 
It's a matter of idea,
Not in glory the essence. 
They follow her,
And they carry glory.
