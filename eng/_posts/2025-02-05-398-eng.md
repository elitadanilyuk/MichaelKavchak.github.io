The Golden Iceberg with the Peak of Faith —
In fact, the mountain 
Precious stones
Grew to the bottom.

Not a floating splinter
And the protruding base.
Linguoseismologist
I would just say: Word.

Confidence and anxiety 
Abyss and mountains —
Your victory.
Hope and believe.
