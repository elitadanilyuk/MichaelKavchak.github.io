White and black
Explicit and non-existent
Sad and beneficial
Dead and seething.

Relativity is mixed
Filled with shades.
Where it is necessary, hasty,
And somewhere accelerated.

Clamped between the poles
ORBITAL DEPENDENCE
Albeit delimited by the edges,
Doomed to infinity.
