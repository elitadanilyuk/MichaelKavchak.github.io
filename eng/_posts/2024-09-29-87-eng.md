Experienced 
A rich sacrifice.
Noticed: 
And guilty in the end.

Timeless
As if it were not —
They made peace with the enemy. 
And a friend is no longer a friend —
They quarreled with him.

Who gave more, 
Moreover, more was requested.
If the pace slowed down —
They were angry with him.
