A person is learning
Still alive.
Zach won't leave
Her soul.
 
The Spirit is the teacher, 
The body is a vessel,
The soul of the wearer.
We judge the body by the soul.
 
The body of the soul will leave, 
He will come home,
He will meet his father
The exam will pass.
