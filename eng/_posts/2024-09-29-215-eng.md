Torture the cat —
Show love. 
They annoy you —
So they love
(In order to add 
Or using, 
They will perish).

The two ends also 
In love.
Courtship or
The shedding of blood. 

Loving, sacrificing 
And demanding 
Victims. 
Obtaining love,
Prepare and die.
