Doubtful you see.
You never know
Where will you lose, 
And where do you stumble. 

It was as if I met a friend —
He became the enemy.
Grab a grudge —
God gave me happiness.

Rooted Fate
Ready for anything.
Will rejoice in the will,
Slavery will also be destroyed.
