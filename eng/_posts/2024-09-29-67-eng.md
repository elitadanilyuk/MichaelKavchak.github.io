The bones are already sore
Tired of lying 
I've had enough rest 
It's time to get up. 

Dedicated to business
Turn the mountains
Till he fell, 
There was no grief.

But it is also good to be sick.
The thought works 
In the fog or thirsty
He talks about eternity.
