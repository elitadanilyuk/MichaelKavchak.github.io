Love is perfect 
Time tested,
Betrayal did not know 
(Or hid well),
Mutually raised.
 
Accepted disagreements
As a blessing, 
Swear of freedom
And enjoyment
Instead of patience. 
 
Love is perfect 
I overcame everything
The temptations of the past
Grief did not know.
... Because life was loved.
