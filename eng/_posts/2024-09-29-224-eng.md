Awakened by the idea
Born in thought
Expressed in sound
In the envelope is sealed 
Drawn in a letter.

Consent or coerced,
Construction or destruction,
Absorbing or shaking —
Materialization.

Denial or approval,
When they say: Done.
Fullness and glorification —
WORD!!!
