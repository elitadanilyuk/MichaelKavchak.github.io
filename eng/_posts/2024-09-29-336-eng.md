Clay stucco
I cut it out of stone.
WOOD SHAVINGS —
The need for the real.

I look in the storm
In the sun and in the moon,
In length and weight
In the void and in quantity.

At the point of intersection 
He told me
Going inside:
“Well done, what I was looking for.”
