Who speaks Russian,
“Russian” becomes. 
The orc does not capture, but “liberates”
Takes his,

Where are others, where are ours —
It's easy to figure it out:
The language of “Rashi” —
And this is enough.

Go to her
And call trouble.
You will soon hear from the trench:
“Let's go!”
