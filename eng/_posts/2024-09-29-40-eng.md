When you warm your chest
Blankets on the bedspread
Colds —
Also good in the Lord,

And also on the weekend
On New Year's Eve 
Comes in a dream 
A remarkable thing. 

You take the phone 
And with poems you write —
Marathon thought
You will even leave it to others.
