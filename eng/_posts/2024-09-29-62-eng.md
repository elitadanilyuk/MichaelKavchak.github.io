Returned from captivity
At the beginning of the war —
You go home,
Where is the woman and the sons. 

When they let go,
As the war broke out,
Glad they didn't kill.
But they met sadly.

If it was delayed,
He's back, and in the mood.
Everyone turned away —
Traitors in battle.
