An important point
Intuition will choose the style.
From the inside the words flow 
Rust disappears and rot. 

Yamb, or Choir,
Freestyle or canon —
My Primitive 
Without obstacles. 

The soul was torn — 
He let go. 
I didn't care — 
Yamb or Choir.
