To live fully — 
Appreciate processes
Do not rush to the end 
Do not drive — DO NOT.
 
Goal to choose 
Héattainable.
Fold by particles 
Valyazhno. 

And to the descendants,
To continue,
For them to live,
To multiply,
In order not to finish.
