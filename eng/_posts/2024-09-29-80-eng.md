Life is a scale
Effort — steering. 
Truth is completeness 
Absence is zero. 

Movement — while you are walking
Caught — discord. 
You seek — you live.
Achieved — means dead. 

Perfect is unattainable.
The good is limitless —
Evil will prove.
Careful!!!
