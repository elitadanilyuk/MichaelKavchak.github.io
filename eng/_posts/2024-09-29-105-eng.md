In the picture
It turns out even better. 
Because life is routine
Laid to the finish line.
 
The artist understood
Filtered
The most important thing in a nutshell
He put it on the canvas.
 
Chaff sifted
Wrinkles removed
Where necessary, reinforced,
Where necessary, hide.
