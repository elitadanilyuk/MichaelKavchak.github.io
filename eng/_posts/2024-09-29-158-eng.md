Jealous and envious, 
God is only jealous. 
About human conversion.
His repentance worries us.
And envy yourself 
He has no reason.
 
For He is the ruler of the world, 
We are His property.
Can the owner
Fall in envy
 
To yourself? Whether to steal
In himself?
Or put
Not there,
Where is He needed?
