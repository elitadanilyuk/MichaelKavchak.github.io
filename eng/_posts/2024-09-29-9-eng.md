I still don't quit 
How can 
To love Ukraine —
“With deep feelings” 
In Russian, to drown. 

What kind of Ukrainian 
With the tongue of a katsa? 
Who will replace the mother 
Father and brother?

Although it is not bad,
“What a difference” will say
About the stranger, even the enemy,
Will he fall under the oppressor?
