Her craving for her husband
Love calls her
Behind her only, 
Because her craving for her husband.

Her craving for her husband
With the nature of glue,
To draw to her,
Because her craving for her husband.

Her craving for her husband 
To have a husband.
Her craving for her husband 
To dominate it.
