They rob the population,
To replenish wealth
“Celestials of Terema”.

For this reason, the Maidan 
In Zaporizhia genes,
To break the shackles 
In corrupt schemes. 

Tax Required 
For the good of the people.
Uncontrollable — false
Causes damage.
