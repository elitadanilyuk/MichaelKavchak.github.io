Peignoirs and accessories 
For fixing and removing. 
The excitement of the pioneers,
Attributes of love.

Mystery Holders
Self suggestion,
Erotica in the industry
For the buyer to light up,
Seller to get rich.

To the top of achievements 
The last obstacle.
To concealments and penalties
Nature calls.
