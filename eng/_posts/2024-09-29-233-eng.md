Gone are the waters
All ran away,
Generations began
Aren't you late?

In your hands
Two lives,
Where there, three!
Does not hold the groin
And you still have to carry!

The two of them and themselves
In the operating room.
Here is the breakthrough...
...
Lest it be too late...
