The smart one knows 
The wise man uses.
Knowledge begins, 
Knowledge ends. 
 
Knows sperm 
How to Fertilize,
Does the asteroid know,
Like burning to the point. 
 
The beginning knows
How to become a finish.
The living dies, 
Because he knows
How to resurrect.
